About

The Bay Seasoned Vodka captures the spirit of the chesapeake bay

The Bay Seasoned Vodka is a four times distilled vodka highlighted by a blend of classic seafood seasonings familiar to lovers of the eastern shore for generations.

Notes of celery seed, black and red pepper, nutmeg, cardamom and other proprietary spices blend seemlessly with sea salt and our handcrafted vodka.

The result is a smooth and savory vodka
